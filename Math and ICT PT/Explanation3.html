Dependent and Independent Variables


Independent Variable - This is a variable that can stand alone. It refers as the input of the function. (Cause) Dependent Variable - This is a variable that cannot stand alone. It depends on other factors. It refers as the output of the function. (Effect)

1. The balloon filled with yeast and sugar expands better in a hotter temperature.
Independent Variable: Temperature
Dependent Variable: Ballon filled with yeast and sugar expanding

2. The amount of citric acid you put in a juice mixture affects how sour it is.
Independent Variable: Amount of citric acid
Dependent Variable: How sour the juice mixture is

3. Jungkook spent an hour studying for an upcoming Math test; his score will determine how well he studied.
Independent Variable: The time Jungkook spent to study
Dependent Variable: The score he will get on the quiz
